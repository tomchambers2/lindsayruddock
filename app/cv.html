Name: Lindsay Ruddock

Profession: Design Engineer in electronics / hardware / systems

Qualification: B.Sc. in Electronic and Electrical Engineering (UMIST)

Personal profile

An experienced innovative hard working analog and digital electronics, hardware and embedded systems design engineer, 30 years experience of design and specification in the aerospace, railway, automotive, defence, nuclear and communications industries.

Primary skills

Analog Electronics - DC upwards, circuit design and analysis, low noise, communications, signal processing, power supplies, filters, switched capacitor, sensors, interfaces, power electronics, BDCM drive.

Hardware – High speed signal design, remote interface units, signal integrity (SI), microcontrollers and interfacing. High speed mixed signal.

VHDL, logic design, DSP techniques, displays, touch screens, FPGA specification.

System design - embedded real time system hardware architecture, embedded software engineering using structured design and methodology, extensive assembler, C for embedded microcontrollers, system design and simulation.

EMC – Expert engineering knowledge for successful circuit and systems design.

Test methods, test plans and testing. MILSTD461, EN50121-3-2 & EN50155, DefStan 59-41, DO160, Automotive standards.

Extensive experience of leading the identification and solution of complex EMC issues.
